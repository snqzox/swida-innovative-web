How we work

Six steps where Swida
does not feel like a forwarder.

From the moment your request lands to the dashboard that closes the loop — every step has a system behind it. Data, APIs, and AI, end-to-end.

01 · Request

AI parses the request, instantly.

Book a truck like an e-shop with Instacarrier — or send us a free-text request and our AI parses it into a structured shipment, ready to price. Either way, no back-and-forth.

📩 incoming · accounts@client.tld "Need urgent van from Bratislava to Lyon tomorrow morning, ~1.2 t, dimensions..." → AI parse ✓ structured shipment → geocode ✓ BTS · LYS · 1 420 km → price → € 1 240
02 · Pricing

Market-aware, every quote.

Every price is shaped by historical lane data, route economics, and a market-aware model — not a coordinator's gut feeling. Binding price, in under a minute.

POST /instacarrier/quotes { "from": "Bratislava, SK", "to": "Lyon, FR", "kind": "van_3_5t", "kg": 1200 } 200 OK → € 1 240 · binding · pickup in 2 h
03 · Carrier sourcing

Posted once. Locked in minutes.

Connected to every major European load exchange. We post the demand once, negotiate fast, and lock the carrier with a binding rate — your coordinator just confirms.

[10:14] post to timocom · trans.eu · teleroute [10:21] 3 carriers responded [10:24] best rate matched, contract sent [10:31] driver assigned · CMR generated ✓
04 · Monitoring

Tracking pushed, not asked.

We pull GPS from the carrier's telematics provider on a schedule and push fresh location data into your TMS — or send a structured status email if you prefer ops by inbox.

[14:02] shipment #SWD-9821 · A4 near Brno [16:14] shipment #SWD-9821 · D1 → Plzeň [18:30] shipment #SWD-9821 · DE border on schedule
05 · Invoicing

Closing the loop, automatically.

The moment delivery is confirmed, your invoice is generated and dispatched — by email, structured PDF, or full EDI integration. One enterprise client of ours hasn't seen a manual freight invoice in over a year.

// on delivery.confirmed → render invoice (PDF + EDIFACT INVOIC) → send to accounts@client.tld → post to /ap/ingest ✓ → carrier invoice auto-matched · 80% straight-through
06 · Reporting

Your freight, in a dashboard.

See every shipment, every cost line, every on-time stat. Loyalty points you can spend on merch and perks. Lane analytics that let you plan smarter contracts next quarter.

How we operate

The boring parts of freight,
handled by software.

Most forwarders run on email threads and spreadsheets. We run on a pipeline — every step has a system behind it, and the humans focus on the calls that actually move freight.

  1. 01
    Request comes in

    AI parses your email or portal request, drafts a structured shipment record, and proposes a market-aware price — your coordinator just confirms.

    AI parsing
  2. 02
    Capacity is sourced

    Connected to every major European load exchange. We post once, negotiate fast, and lock the carrier with a binding rate.

    Exchange API
  3. 03
    Truck is dispatched

    Driver gets the order on their device. CMR generated automatically. Pickup window confirmed back to you before the wheels start turning.

    Automation
  4. 04
    Position is monitored

    An AI agent pulls GPS from the carrier's telematics provider every 2–4 hours and pushes it to your TMS or inbox. No "where is my truck?" emails.

    Real-time tracking
  5. 05
    It bills itself

    Delivery confirmed → invoice raised → sent over EDI or email. Carrier invoices auto-matched, posted, and reconciled — 80% straight-through.
